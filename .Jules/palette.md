## 2024-05-24 - Add accessible close buttons to modals
**Learning:** Found a pattern across multiple modal components (`EditTripModal`, `LuggagePickerModal`, `InventoryPickerModal`) where icon-only close buttons lacked `aria-label`s and the modals lacked `Escape` key close handlers.
**Action:** When creating or editing modals in the future, ensure that icon-only buttons have descriptive `aria-label`s and that keyboard accessibility (like the Escape key) is implemented to close the modals.
## 2024-05-25 - Ensure keyboard discoverability for hover-revealed action buttons
**Learning:** Found a pattern where secondary action buttons (like edit/delete on trip cards) were hidden behind `opacity-0 group-hover:opacity-100`. While they were in the DOM and could receive keyboard focus via tabbing, they remained visually invisible when focused if the mouse was not hovering over the container, making them undiscoverable for keyboard-only users.
**Action:** When implementing hover-revealed action groups, always add `focus-within:opacity-100` to the parent container that has `group-hover:opacity-100`. Additionally, explicitly add `focus-visible:ring-2` (and `focus-visible:outline-none`) to the individual buttons to ensure clear focus rings when they do become visible.
